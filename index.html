About Me

I'm Emily, a convergence journalism student at the University of Missouri! I have a passion for telling stories without visuals. The first time I listened to a podcast, I was overwhelmed with the emotion I felt. I was fascinated with the idea that I could have such a strong emotional response to something I couldn’t see. After graduation I hope to write and produce long-from audio pieces or podcasts. We all deserve a chance to share our stories, and I want to be someone who can give my community a voice.
